Raya Electronics

Since its establishment, Raya Electronics has demonstrated its resounding success as a market leader in consumer electronics and IT Products services and products, at the level of retail, e-commerce, and especially corporate business. Raya Electronics offers a premium shopping experience through our operation of over 131 retail outlets, covering numerous districts and areas in Egypt. This broad network of outlets involves 72 retail shops, including partner branded stores and 15 megastores, 18 Raya stores in malls, shopping centers, 10 i2 stores, 15 Samsung shops and 12 HUAWEI shops, and 59 Etisalat franchises and mini franchises. There we offer a full range of customer electronics, services, and after sales support under one roof. We ensure that our customers enjoy a unique shopping experience, flexible installment programs, easy payment options and after sales services while having an easy access to a wide range of high-quality electronics, home appliances, TVs, mobile phones and accessories, tablets, laptops, printers, and cameras and white goods of leading international brands.

Stores and Expansions

Raya always strives to expand its presence and operations in order to cover every nook and cranny. Meanwhile, its notable presence is not limited to its retail offline stores, as it also developed its online presence, launching a user-friendly application and revamping its online shopping portal Rayashop.com which offers a 24/7 availability and a wider variety of product choices, including electrical appliances and beauty products and affordable fast delivery service to all governorates. The retail operations are fully automated through Microsoft Retail Management System and Oracle Financials to ensure controls, stock monitoring and product availability.

i2 Stores

In 2020, Raya Trade has made great strides towards becoming the largest distributor of electronics in Egypt by acquiring 100% of the shares of i2 stores, the leading distributor of mobile technology and wireless services in Egypt, after a great deal of 185 million pounds. Through i2, we provide our dear customers with the most prominent electronics, including phones, computers, tablets and accessories of exceptional quality and much more, besides the best maintenance services and after-sales support.

Raya Plus – Loyalty Program

With Raya, your enjoyment when you shop goes beyond your shopping experience and even our top-notch after-sales services. With Raya Plus loyalty program, you can collect points on every purchase and earn great discounts at various stores within the broad network of Raya’s partners. All you have to do is sign up on Raya’s app or website, and enjoy the inviting online shopping experience on its platforms. With every purchase, you will get points to redeem after one month of purchase - valid for 1 year. Raya partners’ umbrella includes prominent stores in food and beverage, fashion, entertainment, health, transportation and supermarkets.
