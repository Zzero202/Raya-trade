Raya Smart Care

About Raya Smart Care

Raya Smart Care services started as the first multi-brand mobile customer service center in Egypt. Encompassing a wide portfolio of Mobile brands Nokia, SAMSUNG, Huawei, Lenovo, Motorola, ZTE, Asus, and many more. Backed up with an ISO 9001:2008, 14001:2004, OHSAS 18001:2007 certified operations; Raya Smart Care efficiently serves beyond 28,000 customers per month through a network of over 60 touch Points all over Egypt. A work force of highly skilled and passionate experts who strive to introduce a special customer experience throughout the service centers. Raya Smart Care service reflects the highest technical levels of repair in an ultra-modern repair factory with 100% compliance with brands’ standards. In line with Raya’s vision of diversifying the portfolio within its operations; Raya Smart Care services expanded its portfolio to include consumer electronics and IT Products services.
